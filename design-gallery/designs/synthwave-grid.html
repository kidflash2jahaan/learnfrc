Everything you need to actually learn a department — not just skim it. Free, forever, and built by students who competed.

Written guides & lessons

Clear, sequential guides for every department, written to be read on a phone in the pit or at the shop bench.

Quizzes that check you

Short quizzes after each lesson so you know a concept stuck before you rely on it during build season.

Printable certificates
