Assembly steps · what you get

Read, practice, prove it.

Everything on LearnFRC is free and self-paced. Start anywhere on the sheet and work through the drawing at your own scale.

STEP 01

Written guides

Clear, illustrated lessons for every department — from your first kickoff to swerve tuning and Impact essays.

STEP 02

Quizzes

Short checks at the end of each lesson so a concept sticks before you carry it into the pit.

STEP 03

Certificates

Finish a track and print a certificate for your binder, your resume, or the team wall.

STEP 04

Guided paths

Ordered routes — like Rookie Build or Drive Team ready — that string lessons into a season plan.

STEP 05

Leaderboard

Track your progress dashboard and climb a friendly leaderboard as you complete lessons and quizzes.
