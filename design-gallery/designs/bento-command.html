What you get, all free

Read a guide, prove it with a quiz, follow a path, and print the certificate — no account required to start.

Written guides & lessons

Plain-language explainers on every subsystem and role, from your first gearbox to your Impact submission — no paywall, no login wall.

Quizzes that check you

Short checks after each lesson so you know the concept stuck before your team leans on it during build season.

Guided learning paths

Ordered sequences that take a new member from zero to contributing in their department, one step at a time.

Printable certificates

Finish a path and print proof of what you learned — for your binder, your team, or your college applications.

CERTIFICATE · LEARNFRC

One per completed department path. Yours to keep, always.

Progress dashboard & leaderboard

Track finished guides across your season and see how your effort stacks up. A glossary and a command-style terminal round out the toolkit for the students who like to move fast.
