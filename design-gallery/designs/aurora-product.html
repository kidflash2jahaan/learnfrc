What you get

A path from rookie to competition.

Read a guide, prove you learned it, and keep a record you can show a mentor. Everything is free — the certificates, the paths, the leaderboard, all of it.

step 01

Written guides & lessons

Clear, illustrated explanations for every department — from reading a wiring diagram to running a scouting match. No paywall, no login to read.

step 02

Quizzes that check you

Short quizzes at the end of each lesson confirm you understood the material before you move on to the next guide in a path.

step 03

Printable certificates

Pass a department and print a certificate — proof for a mentor, a portfolio, or your own record of everything you've mastered.

step 04

Guided learning paths

Follow a curated sequence — rookie onboarding, build-season prep, drive-team readiness — instead of guessing what to read next.

step 05

Progress dashboard & leaderboard

Track completed guides across departments on your dashboard, and see where you stand on the community leaderboard.

step 06

Glossary & command terminal

Look up any FRC term in the glossary, or navigate the whole site with a command-style terminal built for people who like to type.
