02 / Method

What you get, every time

Read a guide, check what you learned, prove it, and track how far you've come.

Read

Written guides & lessons

Clear, illustrated writing for every department — the kind of explanation a good mentor would give, free to read with no login.

Check

Quizzes that check understanding

Short quizzes at the end of a lesson tell you whether it stuck — and point you back to the exact section if it didn't.

Prove

Printable certificates

Finish a department and print a certificate you can hand to a mentor or add to a portfolio. Proof you did the work.

Follow

Guided learning paths

Not sure where to start? Follow a path — an ordered route through the guides tuned for rookies, coders, or drive team.

Track

A progress dashboard

See which guides you've read, which quizzes you've passed, and how far along you are in every department at a glance.

Compete

Glossary & leaderboard

Look up any FRC term in the glossary, and climb a friendly leaderboard as you finish lessons across the platform.

03 / Sequence

How a student uses it

Four steps from "I just joined" to "I know my department."

Step 01

Pick a department

Open the index above and choose where you fit — or where you want to grow.

Step 02

Read the guides

Work through lessons in order, or jump to the exact topic you need right now.

Step 03

Pass the quizzes

Confirm what you learned. Optionally make an account to save your progress.

Step 04

Earn the certificate

Complete a department, print your certificate, and move on to the next one.
