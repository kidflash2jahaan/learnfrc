How it works

Three steps, kickoff to comp.

STEP 01

Find your department

Open the guide for the craft you do — or the one you're curious about. Reading is free and needs no account.

STEP 02

Follow the path

Work through the guided path lesson by lesson, taking the quiz at the end of each to lock it in.

STEP 03

Earn your certificate

Finish the department, print the certificate, and watch your progress and rank update on your dashboard.
